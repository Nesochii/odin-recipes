Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small tortillas filled with a variety of proteins, vegetables, and toppings. Theyâ€™re customizable, quick to prepare, and perfect for weeknight dinners or gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef (or chicken/pork)
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade mix)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar or Mexican blend cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • Sour cream and salsa (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and a splash of water, simmer for 5 minutes until well combined.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until soft.
  4. Assemble tacos by filling tortillas with seasoned meat, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream, salsa, or your favorite toppings.
  6. Serve immediately while warm.
