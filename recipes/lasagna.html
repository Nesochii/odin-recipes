lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish, made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel (or ricotta), and plenty of cheese—usually mozzarella and Parmesan. It’s hearty, comforting, and served as a main course.

ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook the beef in a skillet.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, beef, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
