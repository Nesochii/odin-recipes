Pasta

Description

Pasta is a versatile Italian staple made from wheat flour and water. It can be paired with a wide variety of sauces, proteins, and vegetables. This simple recipe focuses on a classic tomato-based pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g dried pasta (spaghetti, penne, or fusilli)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  4. Pour in chopped tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  5. Drain pasta and add it to the sauce. Toss well to coat.
  6. Season with salt and pepper.
  7. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan if desired.
