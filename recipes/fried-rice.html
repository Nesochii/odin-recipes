fried-rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and versatile dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with vegetables, protein, and seasonings. It’s a perfect way to use up leftovers and customize with your favorite ingredients.

ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add diced onions and cook until softened.
  3. Push onions to the side, pour in the eggs, and scramble until set.
  4. Add the cooked rice and mixed vegetables, stir-frying to combine.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and sesame oil, mixing well.
  6. Cook for another 2–3 minutes until heated through.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
